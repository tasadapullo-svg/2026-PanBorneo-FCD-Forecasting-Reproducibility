# Variable Definitions

- speed: observed traffic speed after processing.
- free-flow speed: reference speed under uncongested conditions.
- travel time: time required to traverse a segment or interval.
- forecast horizon: future interval predicted by the model.
- input history length: past hours used as model input.
- MAE: mean absolute error.
- RMSE: root mean squared error.
- MAPE: mean absolute percentage error.
- R2: coefficient of determination.
- missingness level: artificial input missingness used in robustness testing.
- noise level: artificial input noise used in robustness testing.
- training ratio: chronological fraction of the training period used.
- small-sample sensitivity: performance change under limited training data.
